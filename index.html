Our Story

Born from a love for tradition & flavor.

La Reina Margaritas is a celebration of culture and royalty — born from the belief that every guest deserves to feel like a king or queen. Our cantina brings together handcrafted shaken margaritas, elevated modern Mexican dishes, and Ozark favorites in one warm, vibrant space.

Inspired by tradition and crafted with love, every plate and every drink is prepared with fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and a touch of elegance — just for you.

Royal Specialties

Dishes fit for royalty.

Bold, traditional, and prepared with the finest ingredients. Each dish tells a story of Mexican heritage reimagined with care.

Signature
Burrito Mexicano Ver platillo ✦

Burrito Mexicano

Large flour tortilla filled with chicken fajita, sautéed onion, and red bell pepper. Smothered in salsa verde, cheese sauce, and roasted salsa roja. Served with rice & beans.

Must Try
Quesabirrias Ver platillo ✦

Quesabirrias

Our spectacular quesabirrias — tender, slow-braised birria, melted cheese, and a rich consomé for dipping. A blend of Mexican flavors you simply can't miss.

Traditional
Mole Poblano Ver platillo ✦

Mole Poblano

Slow-cooked chicken smothered in rich mole made with roasted chiles, chocolate, nuts, and aromatic spices. Served with rice, borracho beans, and warm tortillas.
